Introduction

The IEEE student chapter of the IEEE Student Branch, Jaypee University of Information Technology, Waknaghat, is organising "Research and Development Expo (R&D Expo)" on May 7, 2016. This exhibition is aimed at stimulating and fostering student research. It would provide a forum for exchange of new ideas highlighting the student research accomplishments. It welcomes all the research work, paper and project presentation.
